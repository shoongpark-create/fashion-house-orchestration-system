# Fashion Video Production Guide

`kie-video-generator`를 활용해 브랜드 무드를 전달하는 패션 필름과 바이럴 숏폼 영상을 제작하는 가이드입니다.

## Fashion Video Types

### 1. Fashion Film (Campaign)
*   **Goal**: 브랜드 아이덴티티 전달, 시즌 무드 표현
*   **Prompt Key**: Cinematic, Slow motion, Emotional lighting
*   **Duration**: 15s - 30s

### 2. Lookbook Video (Product)
*   **Goal**: 착용 핏과 소재의 움직임(Drape) 전달
*   **Prompt Key**: Model walking, fabric movement, 4k detail
*   **Duration**: 10s - 15s

### 3. Viral Short (SNS)
*   **Goal**: 시선 집중, 트렌드 반영
*   **Prompt Key**: Fast pace, dynamic transition, vertical format
*   **Duration**: 5s - 10s (Loop)

## Prompt Templates

### Fashion Film Template
```
"Cinematic fashion film shot of a cool model wearing [Neon Pink Hoodie],
walking confidently in [Seoul neon street at night],
slow motion, wind blowing hair,
emotional lighting, depth of field,
high production value, 4k resolution"
```

### Product Detail Template
```
"Macro video shot of [Heavyweight Cotton Fabric],
camera panning slowly across the texture,
showing raw edge details,
soft studio lighting, 4k, sharp focus"
```

### Dynamic Short Template
```
"Fast paced fashion edit,
quick cuts of model poses wearing [Wacky Willy Collection],
dynamic camera movement, zoom in/out,
trendy transition, vertical video style,
high energy vibe"
```

## Platform Guidelines

### Instagram Reels / TikTok
*   **Ratio**: 9:16 (Vertical)
*   **Key**: 첫 3초 안에 시선을 끌어야 함 (Hook)
*   **Style**: Lo-fi, Handheld 느낌도 허용 (Authenticity)

### YouTube / Brand Site
*   **Ratio**: 16:9 (Horizontal)
*   **Key**: 고해상도, 안정적인 카메라 워킹
*   **Style**: Cinematic, Professional

## Usage
이 스킬은 `art-director`와 `social-media-manager`가 영상 콘텐츠 기획 시 참조합니다.
